Our purpose

Pharmeasy is an app aimed to facilitate refills for both patients and pharmacies. This service comes alongside the French government's initiative to launch e-prescriptions nationwide. Acting as a secure bridge connecting registered patients and pharmacists, our app analyses the patientsâ€™ e-prescriptions to set timely refill reminders. On one hand, it helps patients with chronic diseases or long-term treatments to better manage their health. On the other hand, the refill dates are compiled into stock forecasts sold to the partner pharmacies.
